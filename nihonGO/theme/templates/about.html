Why nihonGO?

This semester, we took on the challenge of creating an ambitious web app that showcased our technical skills and ability to collaborate as a team. By harnessing Alex’s love for Japanese, Aiden’s creative vision, Michael’s ambition, & Eunice’s cybersecurity expertise, we brought nihonGO to life.

nihonGO is more than a language learning platform — it’s a bridge connecting English & Japanese speakers offering a space for cultural exchange, language practice, and mutual understanding.
